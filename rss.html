New to RSS? RSS is like an email inbox for blogs and websites you read. Subscribe to feeds by copying their feed URL into your RSS reader of choice. Like email, you can choose which app/website to read your feed in. I suggest NetNewsWire and Thunderbird. Often, you can simply copy the base URL of the website (e.g., https://www.lairdstewart.com) into your RSS reader.
